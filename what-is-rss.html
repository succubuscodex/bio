what is rss?

RSS stands for really simple syndication. It is essentially a link you can paste into a feed reader to keep up with your favourite blogs and newsletters.

Currently my RSS just links to my newsletter

RSS is a great way to keep up with your favourite creators without logging onto platforms that you don't want to use (or if they post to their own site!).

The RSS reader I recommend is tapestry. It lets you link bluesky accounts, youtube as well as standard RSS feeds.
